Washer/Dryer Wrapping & Moving (4:32)
Instructions
  1. Disconnect water supply lines and power cord. Cap open water connections.
  2. Secure the drum — use transit bolts if available, or pack towels inside.
  3. Wrap with moving blankets, secure with shrink wrap (no tape on surfaces).
  4. Use appliance dolly — strap at top and bottom.
  5. On the truck: stand upright, strap to wall. Never lay a washer on its side.
  6. At destination: reconnect, check for leaks, run a test cycle if possible.
  7. For front-load washers: Check for safety bolts on the back.
